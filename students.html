🏆 Student Achievement Spotlight

Blessings Phambala Wins Public Speaking Competition

Blantyre Secondary School is proud to announce that Blessings Phambala, a Form 3 student, has emerged as the champion of the inaugural Blantyre City Public Speaking Competition held at Golden Peacock Hotel. She competed against 14 contestants from 9 schools and claimed the top prize of MWK 500,000.

  • 🥇 1st Place: Blessings Phambala (Blantyre Secondary School)
  • 🥈 2nd Place: Eve Kaime (Swema Private Secondary School)
  • 🥉 3rd Place: Fly Kantefa
