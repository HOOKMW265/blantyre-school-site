About Blantyre Secondary School

"Where Excellence Meets Discipline"

Our Mission & Vision

At Blantyre Secondary School, our mission is to nurture young minds into responsible, knowledgeable, and disciplined citizens. We strive to provide a balanced education that promotes academic excellence, creativity, and moral integrity.

A Legacy of Excellence

Established in 1972, Blantyre Secondary School has stood as a beacon of educational achievement in Malawi. Our dedicated teachers and modern facilities create an environment that fosters innovation, teamwork, and leadership.

We take pride in our diverse student body and our long-standing record of success in academics, sports, and community service.

Our Core Values

Discipline

We believe discipline is the foundation of excellence and integrity in all pursuits.

Integrity

Our students learn to act with honesty, fairness, and respect in all situations.

Excellence

We encourage academic and personal excellence in every student we teach.
