Our History

Blantyre Secondary School was established to provide high-quality education to the youth of Blantyre and beyond. Our commitment to excellence is grounded in discipline, academic performance, and community values.

Our Mission

To nurture disciplined, knowledgeable, and responsible citizens through quality education and mentorship.

Our Vision

To be one of Malawi’s leading secondary schools, recognized for academic excellence, student success, and community service.
