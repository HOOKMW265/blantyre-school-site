Who We Are

Founded in 1940, Blantyre Secondary School is one of Malawi’s most respected academic institutions. Situated in the heart of Blantyre City, we are committed to academic excellence, student growth, and leadership development.

What We Do

We provide a comprehensive secondary education program that emphasizes discipline, innovation, and character development. Our curriculum balances academic achievement with leadership, responsibility, and life skills.

Our Mission

To provide quality education through discipline, hard work, and innovation, nurturing students to become productive members of society.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence and moral integrity that produces future leaders and innovators.
